Challenges Faced by India's Democracy

  • Corruption: Widespread corruption impacts both political and bureaucratic processes.
  • Influence of Money: Money and power often skew electoral fairness and policy decisions.
  • Bureaucratic Inefficiencies: Complex regulations and slow decision-making affect governance and development.
  • Political Instability: Frequent changes in leadership and coalition politics hinder consistent policy implementation.
  • Diversity and Regional Disparities: Addressing the diverse needs of all citizens can be challenging due to regional disparities.
