Political Parties in India

Indian National Congress (INC): Founded in 1885, INC is one of India's oldest political parties. It played a pivotal role in the Indian independence movement and has been a major player in Indian politics since independence.

Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP): Established in 1980, the BJP is a major political party that promotes Hindu nationalism. It has been in power at the national level several times, including the current ruling party as of 2024.

Aam Aadmi Party (AAP): Founded in 2012, AAP emerged from the anti-corruption movement led by Arvind Kejriwal. The party focuses on issues of transparency, corruption, and governance.
